1. Purpose of Swarm Fund

Swarm Fund is a community-driven platform designed to support innovative crowdfunding projects. Our mission is to gather like-minded individuals to discover and promote brilliant ideas. We focus exclusively on non-charitable crowdfunding campaigns that have the potential to benefit independent artists, entrepreneurs, and creators.

2. Email Collection and Use

By submitting your email to Swarm Fund, you agree to receive communications from us, including newsletters, updates on selected projects, and other relevant information. We respect your privacy and will not share your email with third parties without your consent. You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time by following the link provided in the emails.

3. Submission Guidelines

When submitting a crowdfunding project to Swarm Fund:

  • Valid Links: We only accept submissions linked to reputable crowdfunding platforms (e.g., GoFundMe, Kickstarter, Indiegogo).
  • Non-Charitable Projects: We focus solely on non-charitable projects. Submissions related to personal causes, charity, or fundraisers for non-profit organizations will not be considered.
  • Bias and Self-Promotion: Submissions should be genuine and not motivated by personal gain or self-promotion. Any submissions found to be biased or purely self-promotional may be excluded from consideration.

4. Community Standards

We aim to foster a positive and supportive community. Users are expected to:

  • Respect Others: Engage respectfully with other members of the community.
  • Constructive Participation: Provide constructive feedback and avoid negative comments that do not contribute to the growth of the community.
  • Integrity: Maintain honesty and integrity in all submissions and interactions.

Swarm Fund reserves the right to review all submissions and determine which projects will be featured or promoted. Selection is based on the project’s potential, alignment with our mission, and community interest.
